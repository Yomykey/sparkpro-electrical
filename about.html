Our Story

Started With One Van and a Toolbox

SparkPro Electrical was founded in 2011 by Tom Reyes, a qualified, registered electrician who spent a decade working for larger contractors before deciding local homeowners deserved a more personal option. The first year was one van, a pager, and a lot of referrals from neighbours after a job rewiring a 1940s bungalow that three other electricians had turned down.

Fifteen years later, we're still family-run — Tom's still on the tools most days, and the crew he's built is trained the same way he was: do it right the first time, explain it in plain language, and never leave a job site messier than you found it.

We're not trying to be the biggest electrical company in Cannock. We're trying to be the one you'd actually recommend to your neighbour.
